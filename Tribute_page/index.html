Dr. Norman Borlaug
Dr. Norman Borlaug, a plant scientist and humanitarian who was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970
Dr. Norman Borlaug was a plant scientist and humanitarian who developed high-yielding, disease-resistant wheat varieties that helped to prevent mass starvation in developing countries. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his contributions to world peace through increasing food supply.
